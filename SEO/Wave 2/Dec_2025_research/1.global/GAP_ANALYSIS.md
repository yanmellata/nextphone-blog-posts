## Executive Summary

After analyzing all 119 published blog posts across 8 phases, I've identified **7 major keyword gap categories** that represent significant untapped SEO opportunities. These gaps fall into high-intent, low-competition areas where NextPhone can establish authority before competitors.

**Key Finding:** We have excellent coverage of WHAT (AI receptionist features) and WHY (benefits, ROI), but major gaps in HOW (implementation, integrations) and WHO (industry-specific workflows).
