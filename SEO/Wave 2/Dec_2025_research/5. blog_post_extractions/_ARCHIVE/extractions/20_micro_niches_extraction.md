## Strategic Notes

### Highest-Priority Clusters (Based on Volume + ROI)
1. **HVAC** (Keywords 41-45) - HIGH volume, seasonal peaks, premium emergency pricing
2. **Plumbing** (Keywords 46-50) - HIGH volume, 24/7 urgency, water damage = immediate need
3. **Water Damage Restoration** (Keywords 26-30) - Medium volume, disaster-driven, 60-min response SLA
4. **Property Management** (Keywords 6-10) - Medium volume, 40% after-hours inquiries, proven ROI
5. **Towing** (Keywords 16-20) - Medium volume, roadside emergencies, high conversion intent

### Seasonal Content Opportunities
- **Winter:** HVAC heating (#28), plumbing frozen pipes (#30), locksmith emergencies
- **Summer:** HVAC cooling (#29), pest control, water damage (sprinkler/pool issues)
- **Weather-Dependent:** Towing (storms, winter conditions), water damage/restoration (heavy rain)

### Vertical-Specific Insights
- **Funeral Home:** Emotional/high-trust vertical. AI + human backup model. 76% small family businesses = niche targeting.
- **Locksmith/Towing:** Distressed customer states = premium pricing acceptance + immediate need.
- **Property Management:** Workflow automation focus. Tenant screening = operational efficiency angle.
- **Auto Repair:** Peak call patterns (Monday-Tuesday). Intake automation = pre-populated work orders.
- **Pest Control:** Panic-driven calls = professional response improves conversion.

### Content Gap Opportunities (Low Competition)
- Dual-service combinations: "Fire and Water Damage Call Management" (#20)
- Specialist niches: "Preneed Funeral Home Phone Service" (#23), "Commercial Pest Management" (#16)
- Workflow automation: "Mechanic Shop Intake Form Automation" (#26), "Property Management Work Order Dispatch" (#4)
- Emergency screening: "Rental Property Emergency Call Screening" (#5)

### Excluded Per Guidelines
- **Veterinary Emergency (Keywords 1-5):** All 5 keywords excluded due to healthcare/medical vertical exclusion per EXTRACTION_GUIDELINES.md
- No HIPAA, SOC 2, or enterprise compliance topics
- No ServiceTitan integrations
