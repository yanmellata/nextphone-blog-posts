## Search Pattern Analysis

### By Intent Distribution
- **Solution-aware (32 keywords):** Feature validation searches - content strategy: comparison + capability deep-dives
- **Problem-aware (12 keywords):** Pain point identification - content strategy: industry guides + case studies
- **Transactional (6 keywords):** Buying decision - content strategy: pricing, trials, demos

### By User Type Distribution
- **Business owner (19 keywords):** Decision-maker, ROI-focused - messaging: cost savings, lead capture
- **Operations manager (18 keywords):** Process-focused, workflow optimization - messaging: efficiency, automation
- **Office manager (9 keywords):** Daily user, support burden - messaging: ease of use, relief from admin work
- **IT admin (4 keywords):** Technical integration - messaging: API, integrations, security

### Volume Distribution
- **High volume (8 keywords):** Broad, foundational searches - harder to rank but larger addressable market
- **Medium volume (21 keywords):** Sweet spot - feature-specific with meaningful volume
- **Low volume (21 keywords):** Hyper-targeted niches - easier to rank, lower volume but higher intent
