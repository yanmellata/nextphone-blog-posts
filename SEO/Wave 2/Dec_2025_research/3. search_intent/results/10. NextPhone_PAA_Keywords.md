## Executive Summary

This keyword list targets question-format searches in Google's "People Also Ask" boxes. These are real search behavior patterns from business owners, office managers, and operations teams evaluating AI receptionist solutions. The keywords span five critical decision-making areas: implementation, comparison, capability, objection handling, and timing.

**Key Targeting Focus:**
- Service business verticals: HVAC, plumbing, dental, legal, accounting, real estate, contractors
- Decision-makers: Business owners (60%), Office managers (25%), Operations/IT (15%)
- Search intent: 40% transactional/solution-aware (high-intent), 60% problem-aware/informational (nurture-focused)
