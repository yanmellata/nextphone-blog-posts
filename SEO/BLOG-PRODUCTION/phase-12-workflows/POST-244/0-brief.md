## Title
Call Analytics & Reporting Playbook: KPIs Dashboards & Optimization Strategies

## Primary Keyword
call analytics reporting playbook

## Angle (Unique Positioning)

Weekly review checklist, dashboard setup in Google Data Studio, A/B testing greeting scripts, optimization strategies (reduce transfer rate by improving AI training, improve booking rate with better availability communication).
