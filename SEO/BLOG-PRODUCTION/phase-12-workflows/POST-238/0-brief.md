## Title
Call Transfer & Escalation Protocols: When AI Transfers to Humans

## Primary Keyword
call transfer escalation protocol

## Angle (Unique Positioning)
When AI should transfer to human: complex technical questions beyond knowledge base, angry/upset callers requiring empathy, high-value VIP customers (automatic human routing), multi-step troubleshooting requiring back-and-forth, pricing negotiation, contract discussions, specific person request ("I need to speak with John"). Transfer triggers, warm transfer vs cold transfer (AI provides context to human), escalation hierarchy (receptionist → manager → owner), after-hours escalation (when to wake up on-call staff vs schedule callback), transfer analytics (track transfer reasons to improve AI training).
