Handling Callers Who Demand a Live Agent: Scripts & Escalation Logic

## Angle (Unique Positioning)
Post-purchase support content (Priority 79/100). Objection handling for "I want to talk to a real person" scenarios. Escalation strategy framework: immediate transfer (high-value VIP customers, detected frustration), attempt resolution first ("I can help you with that, may I ask..."), explain AI capabilities ("I'm an AI assistant who can schedule appointments and answer questions - what can I help with?"). De-escalation scripts and smooth handoff protocols: preserve context during transfer, warm introduction to human agent, avoid repeated questions. Hybrid AI+human model positioning. Reduces escalation friction.
