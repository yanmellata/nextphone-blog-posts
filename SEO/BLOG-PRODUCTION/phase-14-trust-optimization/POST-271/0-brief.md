## Title
Why Your AI Receptionist Misunderstands Callers & How to Fix It

## Angle (Unique Positioning)
Post-purchase support content (Priority 81/100). Common AI misunderstanding scenarios: regional accents/dialects, industry jargon not in training data, ambiguous requests ("I need help" without context), speaking too fast, background noise interference. Troubleshooting framework: review call transcripts → identify pattern → refine prompts → add examples → test with edge cases. Prompt debugging checklist: add industry-specific terminology, provide context examples, clarify ambiguous phrases. Links to AI training guide, call recording analysis. Reduces support burden, improves customer success.
