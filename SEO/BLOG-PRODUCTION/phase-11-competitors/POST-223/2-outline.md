### KEY TAKEAWAYS (6 bullets)
1. 74.1% of contractor calls go unanswered - any answering service beats voicemail
2. Abby Connect: $299+/mo, 8am-8pm, dedicated team of 5 receptionists

### SECTION 2: Abby Connect (350 words)
- Overview: Las Vegas-based, dedicated team model
- Pricing: $299/mo base, $95 setup, per-call after limit
- Hours: 8am-8pm only (not true 24/7)
- Features: Dedicated 5-person team, Clio/HubSpot/Salesforce integration
- Best for: Businesses wanting consistent team
- Limitation: No night/weekend coverage without add-on

### SECTION 3: VoiceNation (350 words)
- Overview: Per-minute pricing, 24/7
- Pricing: $229/100 min, $2.19/min overage
- Hours: True 24/7, no extra charge
- Features: Bilingual, custom scripts, message delivery
- Best for: Businesses needing true 24/7 with moderate volume
- Limitation: Per-minute costs add up fast

### SECTION 4: Specialty Answering Service (SAS) (300 words)
- Overview: 35+ years, industry-specific scripting
- Pricing: $159/100 min OR $44/mo + $1.54/min pay-as-you-go
- Features: Industry scripts, appointment scheduling
- Best for: Established businesses with specific protocols
- Limitation: Variable costs, complex pricing

### SECTION 5: Posh Virtual Receptionists (250 words)
- Overview: Boutique, Virginia Beach-based
- Pricing: $65/mo + $2.25/min
- Features: Premium service feel, personalized
- Best for: Businesses wanting high-touch service
- Limitation: Per-minute adds up, limited hours

### SECTION 6: AI Receptionist Alternative (400 words)
- Overview: Flat-rate AI answering
- Pricing: $199/mo unlimited typically
- Features: <5 sec answer, 24/7, emergency routing, CRM integration
- Best for: High volume, predictable costs, 24/7 needs
- Limitation: Complex calls may need human backup
- Soft CTA
