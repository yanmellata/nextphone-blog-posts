- SMB owners with aging PBX hardware (5-15+ years old)

- Concerned about downtime during migration
- Want to know total cost comparison (not just monthly fees)
- Looking for validation that migration is worth it

- "What happens if our phone system goes down during migration?"

6. IT staff time spent on phone system issues
7. Missing calls due to system outages
8. Can't add modern features (mobile app, CRM integration)
