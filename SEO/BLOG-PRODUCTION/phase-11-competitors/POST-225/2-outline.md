### KEY TAKEAWAYS (5 bullets)
1. You can keep your RingCentral number and add AI answering on topno switching required
2. Replace "press 1 for sales" IVR with conversational AI via simple call forwarding
3. Works for branch offices, after-hours coverage, or overflow during busy periods
4. Setup takes minutes using RingCentral's built-in call forwarding features
5. AI handles routine calls while routing emergencies and complex requests to your team
