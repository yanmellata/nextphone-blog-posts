## SECTION 3: WHAT IS A CALL ANALYTICS DASHBOARD?

**Purpose:** Define call analytics dashboards and what they track
**Word Count Target:** 300-350 words

**H2:** What Is a Call Analytics Dashboard?

**H3 Subsections:**
1. More Than Just a Call Log
2. What a Dashboard Tracks
3. Enterprise vs Small Business Dashboards

**Key Points to Cover:**
- Definition: A call analytics dashboard is a visual tool that tracks and displays key call data - volume, trends, performance metrics
- NOT just a list of calls - shows patterns, trends, actionable insights
- According to [CloudTalk research](https://www.cloudtalk.io/blog/call-center-analytics-dashboard/), "Call center analytics dashboard is a visual reporting tool that gives teams insight into real-time and historical metrics"
- What dashboards track: Total calls, missed calls, answered calls, call duration, peak hours, call sources, caller information, callback requests, emergency/urgent calls
- Enterprise vs SMB: Enterprise dashboards = complex KPIs (AHT, FCR, CSAT, SLA) for 50+ agent teams. SMB dashboards = practical metrics (total calls, missed calls, peak times) for 1-5 person teams
- Focus on what matters for small contractors: visibility, not complexity
