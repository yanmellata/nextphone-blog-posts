**ROI Calculations:**

**Time Savings Calculation:**
- Average voicemail length: 45 seconds
- Listening time includes: Dial-in (15 sec) + prompts (10 sec) + message (45 sec) + replay if unclear (20 sec) = 90 seconds average
- Reading transcription: 10-15 seconds
- Time saved per voicemail: 75 seconds (1.25 minutes)

**For 42 calls/month business:**
- If 74.1% go to voicemail = 31 voicemails/month
- Time saved: 31 × 1.25 minutes = 38.75 minutes/month = **~40 minutes/month**

**For high-volume business (100+ calls/month):**
- If 70% go to voicemail = 70 voicemails/month
- Time saved: 70 × 1.25 minutes = 87.5 minutes/month
- Plus search time saved (finding old voicemails): ~2 hours/month
- Plus re-listening time saved: ~1 hour/month
- **TOTAL: 5+ hours/month saved**
