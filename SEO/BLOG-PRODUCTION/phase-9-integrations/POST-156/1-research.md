## 1.3 USER INTENT & REAL QUESTIONS

**Search Intent Analysis:**
Users searching "Google Voice alternative" are in **consideration stage** - they already use Google Voice (personal or business) and have hit limitations. They're evaluating whether to:
1. Upgrade within Google Voice (Starter ’ Standard ’ Premier)
2. Switch to a different VoIP platform
3. Add AI answering layer on top of Google Voice
4. Fully replace with AI receptionist service

**Common Pain Points (from competitor content & PAA):**

**Voicemail Backlog:**
- "If voicemail has become a backlog rather than a system, you've outgrown Google Voice"
- Missing calls during work = relying on voicemail transcriptions

**Team Growth:**
- "The Starter plan caps you at 10 users, forcing costly upgrades as you grow"
- Need for ring groups, call routing requires $20/month Standard plan

**Integration Needs:**
- "Your phone system is locked into Google's ecosystem, unable to connect with CRMs like HubSpot or automation tools like Zapier"
- Can't automate lead capture from calls

**Professional Features:**
- No toll-free numbers
- No desktop app (mobile/web only)
- Limited call recording (requires upgrade)
- No advanced routing

**Cost Confusion:**
- Must pay for Google Workspace ($7/user/month) PLUS Google Voice ($10-$30/user/month)
- Hidden costs add up: $17-$37 per user per month minimum

**Real User Concerns:**
1. "How do I handle calls when I'm on a job site?"

3. "Do I have to port my number away from Google Voice?"
