3. **Call Quality/Qualification:**
   - "Too many spam calls clog our Aircall queue - can we filter these out?"
   - "Agents waste time on simple questions (hours, pricing) - can AI handle routine inquiries?"
   - "We need to qualify leads before they reach our sales team - what's the best approach?"

4. **Integration Complexity:**
   - "We use Salesforce but also need data in our custom database - can Aircall webhook to multiple systems?"

5. **Feature Limitations:**
   - "Aircall's AI features cost extra - are there better AI options?"
   - "We need appointment booking during calls - does Aircall do this?"
   - "Can Aircall handle callback requests automatically?"

**Inferred Search Intent:**

Users searching "Aircall integration" are typically:
1. **Already using Aircall** and looking to connect it with other tools
2. **Evaluating Aircall** and researching integration capabilities as part of buying decision
3. **Looking for alternatives** to solve specific integration problems Aircall doesn't address
4. **Seeking automation** to reduce manual work and improve efficiency

**Key Questions to Answer:**
