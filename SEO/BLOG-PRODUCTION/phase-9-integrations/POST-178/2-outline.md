## SECTION 4: Three Payment Collection Workflows

**Purpose:** Show flexibility of different payment approaches for different business needs

**Word Count Target:** 250-300 words

**H3 Subsections:**
1. Workflow 1: Full Payment Upfront
2. Workflow 2: Deposit Now, Balance Later
3. Workflow 3: Card on File for Staged Billing

**Key Points to Cover:**

**H3: Workflow 1: Full Payment Upfront**
- **Best for**: Small jobs, service calls, consultations, inspections
- **How it works**: Customer pays full amount during booking call
- **Example**: $350 HVAC diagnostic call, $150 electrical troubleshooting, $500 plumbing service
- **Benefit**: Zero payment risk, immediate cash flow, no collection hassles

**H3: Workflow 2: Deposit Now, Balance Later**
- **Best for**: Large projects, multi-day jobs, material-heavy work
- **How it works**: Collect 20-50% deposit during call, charge balance before/after service
- **Example**: $8,000 roof repair (collect $2,000 deposit during call, $6,000 on completion)
- **Benefit**: Secures customer commitment, covers materials/time investment, reduces no-shows
- **Card on file**: Customer's card stored securely in Stripe for final charge

**H3: Workflow 3: Card on File for Staged Billing**
- **Best for**: Recurring services, maintenance contracts, ongoing projects
- **How it works**: Collect card during initial call, charge as services are completed or on schedule
- **Example**: Monthly HVAC maintenance contract ($150/month), quarterly pool service ($400/quarter)
- **Benefit**: Automatic recurring revenue, no invoicing, customer never misses payment
- **Security**: Card tokenized by Stripe (you never see full number), PCI compliant

**Data/Stats to Include:**
- **20-50% typical deposit range** - Industry standard for projects
- **PCI compliance handled by Stripe** - Security benefit

**Examples/Quotes:**
- Workflow 1 example: Electrician charges $200 for emergency power outage diagnosis, customer pays during call
- Workflow 2 example: General contractor collects $5,000 deposit on $25,000 kitchen remodel
- Workflow 3 example: Plumber sets up $99/month drain maintenance contract with auto-billing
