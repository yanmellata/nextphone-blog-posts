**FAQ #4: Can NextPhone integrate with my CRM like Vonage does?**

**FAQ #5: What happens to my Vonage hardware if I switch?**
Answer: If you're using Vonage-provided IP phones, you'll need to return them or replace them. However, NextPhone is software-based and works via mobile app, desktop app, or SIP-compatible phonesno proprietary hardware required. Many businesses find this more flexible.
**Link:** None

Answer: Most businesses are fully operational within 24-48 hours. The platform includes AI-powered website analysis that auto-extracts your business information, suggested questions, and knowledge base contentminimizing manual setup time.
