## SECTION 3: NextPhone + n8n Integration: Automated Call Workflows

**Purpose:** Show practical implementation of NextPhone + n8n (THE DIFFERENTIATOR)

**Word Count Target:** 300-350 words

**H3 Subsections:**
1. How NextPhone Webhooks Work with n8n
2. Example Workflow: Call to CRM in 3 Steps
3. What Data Gets Captured

**Key Points to Cover:**

**H3: How NextPhone Webhooks Work with n8n**
- NextPhone's AI answers every call in <5 seconds
- During call: AI collects caller info (name, phone, reason, urgency)
- After call: Webhook sends data to n8n
- n8n receives webhook, processes data, triggers actions
- Real-time = no delays, no manual work

**H3: Example Workflow: Call to CRM in 3 Steps**
1. **Call received:** Customer calls your business number
2. **AI captures data:** NextPhone AI asks questions, collects name, phone, service needed, budget
3. **Webhook fires:** Call ends ’ NextPhone sends JSON data to n8n webhook URL
4. **n8n processes:** Receives data, formats it, sends to your CRM (HubSpot, Pipedrive, etc.)
5. **Automated follow-up:** n8n also sends SMS confirmation to customer

**H3: What Data Gets Captured**
- Caller name and phone number
- Call timestamp and duration
- Reason for call (quote, emergency, question)
- Urgency level (routine vs emergency)
- Custom fields you configure (budget, timeline, service type)
- All structured, ready for automation
