## 1.5 INTERNAL DATA (NextPhone Factbase)

**Core Stats to Adapt for Legal Context:**

While our factbase focuses on home services (13,175 calls from 45 contractors), the patterns apply to solo/small law firms:

- **74.1% missed calls** ’ Legal has 35% miss rate (better but still costly)
- **25.4% callback requests** ’ Applies directly to legal consultations
- **15.9% urgency language** ’ Legal has urgent calls (court deadlines, statute of limitations, emergencies)
- **6.2% true emergencies** ’ Legal emergencies = filing deadlines, injunctions, arraignments

**Adapted ROI Calculations for Law Firms:**

**Scenario: Solo Attorney (30 calls/month)**
- 35% missed = 10.5 missed calls/month
- Average case value = $3,500 (conservative for family law/PI)
- Conversion rate = 25% (legal has higher intent than trades)
- **Lost revenue: 10.5 × 25% × $3,500 = $9,188/month = $110,250/year**

**Cost Comparison:**
- In-house receptionist: $45,000/year (legal pays more than trades)
- Traditional answering service (Smith.ai): $500+/month = $6,000/year
- **NextPhone: $199/month = $2,388/year**
- **Savings vs receptionist: $42,612/year (95% reduction)**
- **ROI: Capture even 1 extra case/month = 17.6x return**

**NextPhone Product Capabilities Relevant to Legal:**

**HTTP Webhooks for MyCase Integration:**
- AI collects: caller name, phone, email, case type, urgency level
- Pushes to MyCase via API: creates new contact, adds to CRM pipeline
- Template variables: {{caller_number}}, {{case_type}}, {{urgency}}, {{preferred_callback_time}}

**Call Transfer for Emergencies:**
- AI detects urgency keywords: "court tomorrow", "statute of limitations", "detained", "restraining order"
- Transfers immediately to attorney's cell
- No legal emergency goes to voicemail

**SMS Follow-up:**
- Auto-sends: "Thank you for contacting [Law Firm Name]. We've received your [case type] inquiry and will call you back within [timeframe]."
- Includes booking link for consultations

**Email Notifications:**
- Attorney receives: caller name, phone (tap-to-call), case type, transcript, recording link
- Real-time delivery (no checking voicemail)
