## How AI Qualifies Property Inquiries

### The Real Estate Qualification Workflow

When someone calls about your listing, here's what happens with NextPhone.

The AI answers in under 5 seconds. No rings, no voicemail, no "I'm with a client, leave a message."

It introduces itself: "Hi, you've reached Sarah Martinez with Martinez Realty. I'm her AI assistant. I can help you with property information and schedule a time to connect. Which property were you calling about?"

Then it qualifies the inquiry:

- **Property interest:** "Are you calling about the 3-bedroom on Elm Street, or would you like to discuss other available properties?"
- **Financing status:** "Are you currently pre-approved for a mortgage, or are you in the early stages of exploring financing options?"
- **Timeline:** "Are you looking to move within the next few months, or are you researching for a future purchase?"
- **Property requirements:** "What's most important to you—location, school district, square footage, or something else?"
- **Contact preference:** "What's the best number and time for Sarah to call you back with detailed information?"

[AI reduces lead screening time by 75%](https://convin.ai/blog/how-can-ai-phone-calls-improve-lead-qualification-for-realtors), allowing agents to focus only on qualified, high-intent prospects rather than spending hours qualifying tire-kickers.

### How Calls Get Routed

Not all leads need immediate agent contact.

**High-priority leads** get transferred instantly:

- Pre-approved buyers asking about a specific property
- Sellers saying "we need to list this week"
- Buyers requesting a same-day or next-day showing

The AI says, "Let me connect you with Sarah right now," and transfers the call to your phone. You stay in control of urgent conversations.

**Standard inquiries** get scheduled callbacks:

- Buyers browsing neighborhoods
- People 3-6 months from purchasing
- General market condition questions

The AI schedules a callback time, sends property details via SMS, and logs everything to your CRM. You follow up when convenient, not while showing another home.

**After-hours calls** get captured and confirmed:

Someone calls at 8 PM on Saturday. The AI gathers their information, confirms you'll call back during business hours, and sends a text with property photos and your contact info.

You start Monday morning with qualified leads in your CRM, not voicemails to transcribe.

[Research shows agents utilizing structured qualification fields within their CRM convert 32% more leads](https://callin.io/real-estate-lead-qualification/) than those relying on unstructured notes.

### What Syncs to Your CRM

Every call automatically logs to LionDesk (or your CRM of choice):

- Contact information (name, phone, email)
- Property interest (which listing, specific questions asked)
- Qualification data (budget range, timeline, pre-approval status)
- Full conversation transcript
- Next action (showing scheduled, callback needed, info sent)

No manual entry. No "I'll add that to the CRM later." It happens in real-time while you're focused on the client in front of you.

## NextPhone + LionDesk Integration Setup

### Three Connection Options

**Option 1: HTTP Webhooks (Direct API Integration)**

NextPhone sends call data directly to the [LionDesk API endpoint](https://zapier.com/apps/liondesk-crm/integrations). You configure the webhook URL, add authentication, and map which fields go where (name, phone, notes, tags).

Setup time: 15-20 minutes.

**Option 2: Zapier Integration (No-Code)**

Connect NextPhone and LionDesk through Zapier. When a call ends, Zapier creates a new contact in LionDesk and attaches call notes. No coding required.

Setup time: 5-10 minutes.

**Option 3: Email Notifications + Manual Entry**

NextPhone sends you an email after each call with full transcripts and qualification details. You review and manually add qualified leads to LionDesk.

This reduces automation benefits but works if you want to test before full integration.
